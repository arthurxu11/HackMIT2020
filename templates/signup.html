How to use

1) Enter your full name into the text area

2) Select the pictures of your card. Use at least three pictures in various lighting conditions. Crop the pictures to the edge of the card.

3) Once you submit, you will be redirected to the status page. If it is training, the website is training your card. It will take up to 10 minutes to train.
